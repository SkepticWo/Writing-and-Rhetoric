What Insights Can Be Gained From Comparing The Electric Range Of Vehicles With Their Prices?

Insights

  • The most popular form of electric vehicles is the traditional EV. These vehicles are powered primarily or entirely by electricity.
  • Battery electric vehicles (BEV) are becoming more and more common.
  • Plug in hybrid vehicles (PHEV) are not as common. These vehicles can run off of battery power but still have an internal combustion engine and take gasoline.
  • Fuel cell electric vehicles (FCEV) are the least common type of electric vehicles. These cars run on hydrogen, however, there are not many hydrogen stations to fill up in the United States.
  • EVs, BEVs, and PHEVs are the most popular types because they are able to be plugged in and charged. Much more convenient then FCEVs.
